Help that shows up at the right moment

Sage isn’t always on. It appears after specific attempts and offers layered hints — without ever handing over the answer.

  • Knows the current task and your attempt count
  • Offers 3 levels of help — nudge to scaffold
  • Never reveals the answer — builds your thinking
  • Designed to fade as you grow stronger
